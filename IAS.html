IEEE NSU IAS Student Branch Chapter

About IAS

Alike other technical societies under the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), IEEE Industry Applications Society (IEEE IAS) is also a society which works to make the transition of academics and corporates easier in terms to pursue a better career. 

IEEE North South University, Industry Application Society was formed on 9th June, 2018 as a chapter of IEEE North South University Student Branch. Since day one, the priority of this foundation is to validate the collaboration between students and the corporates. To achieve this target the society members are working with their full potential to set a benchmark for the future. 

The society has also participated successfully on the very first summit of IEEE Bangladesh Section Industry Application Society. Besides organizing events, they also have arranged several workshops which were instructed by professionals from different organizations.

Mission

The goal of IEEE IAS SBC is set to a mark where students and professionals are coming together on a single page to make learning more enjoyable and easier. The members of IEEE IAS SBC are on their journey to ensure the best use of their capabilities and resources to make that dream come true. Since the beginning of IEEE NSU IAS SB Chapter, its members have been actively and enthusiastically bringing into realization workshops on industry applications.

Vision

IEEE NSU IAS SB Chapter aims are :

- The advancement of theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering for the benefit of humanity ;
- The promotion of safe, reliable, sustainable and economical installations;
- The sharing of knowledge in our domains;
- The creation of engineering standards and recommended practices;
- Produce leaders in the field of engineering through the continued support of IEEE NSU IAS;

What is IAS?

The Industry Applications Society(IAS) is a transnational organization and interested in
advancing the theory and practising electrical and electronic engineering in the development,
design and manufacture, application of electrical systems, controls to the processes, equipment
of industry and commerce, promotion of safe, reliable and economic installations. Also,
industry leadership in energy conservation and environment, health and safety issues, and its
membership&#39;s professional development.

Why join IAS?

If your engineering interests are the industrial or commercial sector&#39;s needs, the Industry
Applications Society (IAS) will be a valuable professional connection.
One of the largest particular interest societies within the Institute of Electrical and Electronics
Engineers (IEEE), the IAS focuses specifically on industry and commerce&#39;s unique needs.
IAS is a source of professional power with nearly 14,000 members worldwide. With a network
of over 370 global chapters, regional events, and national and international conferences, the
society keeps members abreast of current technology developments in electricity and
electronics.

What activities do we usually do?

We host many events, seminars, workshops, Tech-talks and weekly meetings throughout
the year on various industry applications. It helps the members to gather more knowledge
about Robotics and Automation. Also, the members and participants can learn new skills and
develop those skills by participating in events.

How to join IEEE NSU IAS SBC?

To join IEEE NSU IAS SBC, you will have to go through a simple evaluation process. For that,
you will have to write us an email answering some easy questions which will be given to you.
